MIGRANT HEALTH PORTAL

  1. Patient Registration:

    • Patients should register as an applicant on the MHU portal.
    • Click "Login" to begin registration.

  2. Check Clinic Availability:

    • After logging in, create a new submission, and select your current location.
    • If clinics are listed, choose one then proceed to steps 3 and 4.
    • If no clinics are listed, proceed with steps A and B before moving on to steps 3 and 4:

    1. HPA Verification:

      • Doctors must obtain mandatory verification from the Health Professions Authority (HPA) before registering.
      • Download the HPA verification form , complete it, and submit the form along with the requested documents to hpaapplications@gov.tc.

    2. Doctor Registration:

      • Doctors should register on the MHU portal and upload the HPA Verification.
      • Click "Login" to begin registration.

  3. Data Input:

    • The doctor inputs the patient's medical data during the appointment.

  4. Payment:

    • Complete the payment to successfully submit the application.
